Spicy Pickled Vegetables

  1. about 2 lbs mixed fresh vegetables such as green beans, carrots, radishes, turnips, bell peppers, cucumbers, fresh ginger root, garlic cloves
  2. 1 tb salt
  3. superfine sugar
  4. 2 tsps sichuan peppercorns
  5. 3-4 fresh hot chilies
  6. 2 tbs distilled Chinese spirits or white rum
