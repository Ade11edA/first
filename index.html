Spicy Pickled Vegetables

Ingredients

  • about 2 lbs mixed fresh vegetables such as green beans, carrots, radishes, turnips, bell peppers, cucumbers, fresh ginger root, garlic cloves
  • 1 tb salt
  • superfine sugar
  • 2 tsps sichuan peppercorns
  • 3-4 fresh hot chilies
  • 2 tbs distilled Chinese spirits or white rum

Steps:

  1. Wash all the vegetables and dry them well. Peel and trim them as necessary, then cut them into uniform shapes and sizes. In a small bowl, mix together the salt, sugar, and peppercorns.
  2. Layer the vegetables and chilies in a jar with the salt mixture, then add the spiris. Seal the jar with an airtight lif, and shake well to mix. Leave in a cool place for 3-4 days in summer, 5-6 days in winter
  3. Serve the vegetables in small bowls, replenishing the jar with fresh vegetables if you like, and adding a little salt. If any white scum appears on the surface of the brine, add a little more sugar and chinese spirits
